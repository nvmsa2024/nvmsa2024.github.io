Welcome to RTCSA 2024

IEEE RTCSA 2024, the 30th IEEE International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Application, will be held in Sokcho, Korea on August 21-23, 2024. It will be co-located with NVMSA 2024. This year is very special in that we are celebrating the 30th anniversary of RTCSA by looking back the 30-year journey from the first RTCSA event in 1995. Sokcho is one of the largest cities on Korea's east coast near beautiful Seorak mountain. You can enjoy the beauty of the beaches and mountains in Sokcho. The conference proceedings will be included in IEEE Xplore, and indexed by EI.
