Welcome to RTCSA 2024

IEEE RTCSA 2024, the 30th IEEE International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Application, will be held in Korea on August 21-23, 2024. It will be co-located with NVMSA 2024. This year is very special in that we are celebrating the 30th anniversary of RTCSA by looking back the 30-year journey from the first RTCSA event in 1995. The conference proceedings will be included in IEEE Xplore, and indexed by EI.

Note: The exact location will be announced soon.
