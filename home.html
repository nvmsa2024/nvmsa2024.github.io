Welcome to IEEE NVMSA 2024

The 13th Non-Volatile Memory Systems and Applications Symposium (NVMSA) is a premier conference for new ideas and research results in the area of non-volatile memory systems and emerging memory technologies. This year, NVMSA will be held in Sokcho, Korea on August 21-23, 2024. This conference is in conjunction with the International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Applications (IEEE RTCSA 2024). Sokcho is one of the largest cities on Korea's east coast near beautiful Seorak mountain. You can enjoy the beauty of the beaches and mountains in Sokcho. The conference proceedings will be included in IEEE Xplore, and indexed by EI.
